About BookHive

Our story, our mission, our love for books

Our Story

BookHive was born in 2024 from a simple idea — every Indian reader deserves easy access to the best books

from around the world, at fair prices, delivered to their doorstep.



Founded in Bengaluru by a small team of book enthusiasts, we've grown into India's go-to destination for fiction,

self-help, biographies, business books, and more. We believe books have the power to change lives, spark new

ideas, and connect people across cultures.

Our Mission


To make great books accessible to every Indian reader. We carefully curate our collection, partner with Indian

publishers, and offer fair prices so that the cost of knowledge never becomes a barrier.



Why Choose BookHive?


  • Curated Selection: Every book in our catalog is hand-picked by our team of avid readers.
  • Indian Focus: We celebrate Indian authors and publishers alongside global bestsellers.
  • Fair Prices: Direct partnerships with publishers mean better prices for you.
  • Free Delivery: Free shipping across India on all orders.
  • Easy Returns: Not satisfied? Return within 7 days, no questions asked.


Our Team


We're a small team of book lovers, designers, and engineers based in Bengaluru. We work every day to bring you a better book-shopping experience. When we're not reading, you'll find us exploring local cafes, debating which novel to read next, or playing with our office cat, Atticus
